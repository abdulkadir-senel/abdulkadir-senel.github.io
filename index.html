About

Abdulkadir Şenel is an undergraduate student in English Language Teaching at Gaziantep University. He previously studied at İzmir Demokrasi University and Akdeniz University. He has practical experience in English language teaching across various institutions. Besides, he worked on several academic study about linguistics and language acquisition.
