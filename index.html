About

Abdulkadir Şenel is an undergraduate student in English Language Teaching at Gaziantep University. He previously studied at İzmir Demokrasi University and Akdeniz University. He has practical experience in English language teaching across various institutions. His academic interests include linguistics and language acquisition.
